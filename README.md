## ‚ö†Ô∏è Key Business Risks Identified

1. **Geographic Concentration Risk**
   - Over-reliance on a small number of states, particularly California
   - Any disruption in top markets could materially impact revenue

2. **Discount Sensitivity**
   - 2012 showed that increased discounting did not translate into higher revenue
   - Highlights the importance of pricing strategy over volume-only growth

3. **Scalability Pressure**
   - Rapid growth in orders and quantity (2013 onward) increases operational demands

---

## üí° Strategic Recommendations

1. **Market Diversification**
   - Expand into **Central region and mid-tier states**
   - Reduce dependency on top 3‚Äì5 markets

2. **Pricing & Discount Optimization**
   - Continue reducing average discount while maintaining demand
   - Introduce targeted promotions rather than broad discounting

3. **Sustainable Growth Planning**
   - Align operational capacity with rising order volumes
   - Use demand forecasts to avoid service degradation

4. **Regional Performance Monitoring**
   - Track revenue contribution and growth by region annually
   - Flag over-concentration early through KPI thresholds
